`hampter` is the parent project context for formula-driven, continuously evaluated behavior systems. In this wiki, `tiaga_face_v0.1` is currently filed as a HAMPTER subproject focused on expressive robot-eye rendering.

- The subproject stores renderer design, DSL philosophy, prompt assets, geometry findings, and workflow rules.
