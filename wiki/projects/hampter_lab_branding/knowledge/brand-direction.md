# Brand Direction

## Active Tone

- Current visual direction: cold, clean, monochrome, lab/corporate
- Avoid warm palettes unless the user explicitly requests a change
- Avoid fake editorial abundance or decorative softness by default

## Messaging Direction

- Present HAMPTER LAB as a research lab exploring how LLMs enter everyday life as systems, interfaces, and physical behaviors
- Keep copy concise, technical, and calm
- Let unusual ideas come from the project content itself rather than from exaggerated marketing language

## Surface Strategy

- The website is currently the main public expression of this brand
- Interactive runtime pages should feel like they belong to the same system rather than separate microsites unless the user explicitly wants a split
